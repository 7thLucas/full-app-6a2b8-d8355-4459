The Foundation

Why this app exists

The Problem

Budget users waste 20+ minutes every meal search

  • — Scattered across Google Maps, delivery apps, review sites
  • — No tool puts affordability first — it's always a filter, never an identity
  • — Students, workers, and families on tight budgets aren't the target — they're an afterthought
The Solution

One app. Budget-first. Sorted two ways.

  • ✓ Open the app → see affordable food spots near your location instantly
  • ✓ Sort by price (lowest first) or by popularity — your choice, your priority
  • ✓ Built for lower-to-mid income users — not adapted for them
